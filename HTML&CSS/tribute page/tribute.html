Neeko Gameplay
  • Neeko is a medium difficulty mage that utilizes deception to make explosive plays
  • Abilities
  • Passive: Inherent Glamour - Neeko can disguise herself as ally champions as well as targetable allied and neutral units
  • Q: Blooming Burst - circular skill shot that triggers up to two additional times by hitting a champion or killing a target
  • W: Shapesplitter - Neeko goes invisible for a second and creates a moveable clone of herself
  • E: Tangle-Barbs - straight line skill shot that roots targets
  • R: Pop Blossom - after a brief channel time Neeko leaps into the air suspending enemies before slamming them down dealing large damage and stunning
  • Neeko is one of few champions that has been played in every role in League of Legends but is commonly associated with mid lane and support
  • Neeko is a mid range mage that excels as a lane bully
  • Historically, items that enable Neeko's ultimate are ideal. Today, rockerbelt is a classic option but originally Hextech-GLP and Twin Shadows was her go to itemization
Neeko's Lore
  • Neeko was born on a remote and largely unknown island, far to the east, where the last members of an ancient vastayan tribe remained isolated from the rest of the world. They were called the Oovi-Kat, and could trace their lineage generation by generation back to the legendary Vastayashai’rei—the ancestors of all vastaya.
  • She developed a fondness for games, hiding trinkets and thoughts to see if others could find them. Her inquisitive nature knew no bounds, and she was pure and innocent in her charmed existence.
  • Catacylsm destroyed Neeko's home and tribe forcing her to flee
  • Surrounded by lush, tropical greenery in the hinterlands beyond Harelport, Neeko grappled with her recent experiences. Here she found Nidalee and their friendship blossomed.
  • The towns and cities, with all their flaws, called to Neeko. Her ancestors came to her in dreams, showing her the pale branches of those dead trees, over and over. The trees needed color, to bloom again—of that much, Neeko was certain. She asked her friend to join her on this new journey, but Nidalee could not be persuaded.
  • Crestfallen, but determined, Neeko set out alone.
